What is Ubiquinone (Coenzyme Q10)?

Ubiquinone, or Coenzyme Q10, is a naturally occurring compound found in almost every cell of the human body. It plays a vital role in cellular energy production and acts as an antioxidant, protecting cells from oxidative damage. Ubiquinone is present in different forms, including ubiquinone-10 (the most common form) and ubiquinone-50.

The Mechanism of Ubiquinone in Skin Health

The skin is the body's largest organ and is constantly exposed to environmental stressors, such as UV radiation, pollution, and toxins. These factors contribute to the production of free radicals, leading to oxidative stress and damage to the skin cells. This is where ubiquinone comes to the rescue. As a potent antioxidant, ubiquinone neutralizes free radicals, reducing oxidative stress and preserving the integrity of the skin.
