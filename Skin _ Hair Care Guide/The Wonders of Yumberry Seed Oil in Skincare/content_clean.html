DIY Yumberry Seed Oil Skincare Recipes

Creating DIY skincare formulations using yumberry seed oil can be a fun and satisfying method to make use of its skin-beneficial characteristics. Here are some easy DIY recipes you can try at home:

  1. Yumberry Seed Oil Facial Serum: Ingredients are 1 tablespoon yumberry seed oil, 1 tablespoon jojoba oil, and 5-10 drops of your favorite essential oil (e.g., lavender, rosehip). Combine yumberry seed oil and jojoba oil in a small glass bottle or dropper bottle. Add 5-10 drops of essential oil for fragrance and additional skin benefits (optional). Close the bottle and shake well to mix the oils thoroughly. Apply a few drops to cleansed face and neck, massaging gently until absorbed. Use daily as part of your skincare routine to hydrate, nourish, and protect your skin.

  2. Yumberry Seed Oil Moisturizing Body Lotion: Ingredients are 1/2 cup shea butter, 1/4 cup yumberry seed oil, 1/4 cup coconut oil, and 10-15 drops of ylang-ylang essential oil (optional for fragrance). In a double boiler, melt shea butter, yumberry seed oil, and coconut oil over low heat until fully melted and combined. Remove from heat and let it cool for a few minutes. Add ylang-ylang essential oil if desired and stir well. Transfer the mixture to a clean glass jar or container. Allow the lotion to cool and solidify. It will become creamy and spreadable. Apply to the body as needed, especially after showering or bathing, to moisturize and soften the skin.

  3. Yumberry Seed Oil Anti-Aging Face Mask: Ingredients are 1 tablespoon yumberry seed oil, 1 tablespoon plain yogurt, and 1 teaspoon honey. In a small bowl, mix yumberry seed oil, plain yogurt, and honey until well combined. Apply the mixture evenly to the cleansed face and neck, avoiding the eye area. Leave the mask on for 15-20 minutes to allow the ingredients to nourish and hydrate the skin. Rinse off with lukewarm water and pat dry. Follow with your regular skincare routine, including moisturizer if needed.
