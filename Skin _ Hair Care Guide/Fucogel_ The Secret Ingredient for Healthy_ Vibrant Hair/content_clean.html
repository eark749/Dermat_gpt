Fucogel Hair Treatments: From Masks to Serums

 

Fucogel hair treatments are a luxurious and effective way to nourish, moisturise, and protect your hair. Here are several popular treatments ranging from masks to serums that utilise the potential of Fucogel to get healthier, more beautiful hair:

  • Fucogel Hair Mask: Ingredients are 2 tablespoons Fucogel, 1 ripe avocado (mashed), 1 tablespoon honey and 1 tablespoon coconut oil. In a bowl, mix the mashed avocado, honey, and coconut oil until well combined. Add the Fucogel to the mixture and blend thoroughly. Apply the mask to clean, damp hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Leave the mask on for 20-30 minutes to allow the ingredients to penetrate the hair shaft.   • Fucogel Leave-In Conditioner: Ingredients are 1/4 cup Fucogel, 1/4 cup aloe vera gel, 1 tablespoon argan oil and 5 drops lavender essential oil (optional for fragrance). In a small bowl, combine the Fucogel and aloe vera gel until smooth. Add the argan oil and lavender essential oil (if using) and mix well. Transfer the mixture to a spray bottle for easy application. After washing and towel-drying your hair, spray the leave-in conditioner onto damp hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Comb through your hair to distribute the product evenly, then style as desired.

  • Fucogel Scalp Serum: Ingredients are 2 tablespoons of Fucogel, 2 tablespoons of jojoba oil, 5 drops of tea tree essential oil and 3 drops of peppermint essential oil. In a small bowl, mix the Fucogel and jojoba oil until well combined. Add the tea tree and peppermint essential oils and stir thoroughly. Using a dropper, apply the serum directly to the scalp, parting your hair as needed to ensure full coverage. Gently massage the serum into your scalp using your fingertips for 2-3 minutes. Leave the serum on overnight for maximum benefits, then wash your hair as usual in the morning.
