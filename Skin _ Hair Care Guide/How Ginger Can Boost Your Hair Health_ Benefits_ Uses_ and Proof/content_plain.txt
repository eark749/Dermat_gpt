Effective Ways to Use Ginger for Hair
Some effective ways to use ginger for hair:
Ginger Hair Oil
: Grate a tiny amount of fresh ginger. Combine the shredded ginger and 2-3 teaspoons of your preferred carrier oil. Warm the mixture slowly (but not to the point of boiling). Once warm, drain the mixture to remove the ginger pulp. Massage the ginger-infused oil into your scalp in circular strokes. Leave it on for 30 to 60 minutes before washing it off with a gentle
shampoo
.
Ginger Hair Mask
: Grate or mix fresh ginger to extract juice. Combine two tablespoons of ginger juice, one tablespoon of honey, and two tablespoons of aloe vera gel. Apply the mixture to your scalp and hair, concentrating on the roots. Leave it on for 20 to 30 minutes. Rinse it completely with lukewarm water and shampoo.
Ginger and Lemon Juice Scalp Treatment
: Extract the juice from a small piece of fresh ginger and combine it with freshly squeezed lemon juice. Apply the mixture to your scalp, concentrating on areas with dandruff or excess oil. Rinse with lukewarm water, and then use a mild shampoo.
Ginger Hair Rinse
: Boil slices of fresh ginger in water (2 cups) for 10-15 minutes. Allow the mixture to cool and filter the liquid. After shampooing, apply the ginger water to your hair as a last rinse. Leave it in; don't rinse it.
Ginger and Onion Juice Hair Growth Treatment
: Extract the juice from a small piece of fresh ginger and a medium-sized onion. Mix the two juices. Apply the mixture to your scalp and massage gently to ensure even dispersion. Leave it on for 30 to 45 minutes. Rinse thoroughly with water and a mild shampoo.
Ginger Tea Hair Mist
: Boil a small piece of fresh ginger in water (1 cup) for 10 minutes. Allow it to cool and filter the liquid. Pour ginger tea into a spray bottle. Spritz it on your scalp and hair roots daily, or a couple times each week.
Ginger and Coconut Milk Hair Mask
: Combine a small piece of fresh ginger and 1/2 cup coconut milk. Apply the mixture to the scalp and hair. Place a shower cap over your hair and leave it on for 30-45 minutes. Rinse with lukewarm water and apply shampoo as usual.
Ginger Oil Treatments for Scalp Health
Ginger oil comes in the form of extracts or essential oils, the latter of which must be diluted with carrier oil before use. Apply the product to your scalp and hair for a spicy, stimulating aroma. Rinse after 15 to 30 minutes.
