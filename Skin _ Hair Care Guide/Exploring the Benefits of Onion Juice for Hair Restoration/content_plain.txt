Onion Juice and Aloe Vera Gel Scalp Treatment

Color-Treated Hair
: Onion juice treatments can be adapted for color-treated hair by avoiding ingredients that may strip or fade hair color. Before using onion juice on color-treated hair, conduct a patch test to ensure that it does not cause discoloration or change the colour. Apply the mixture sparingly to the scalp, avoiding contact with the lengths of hair.
Sensitive Scalp
: Individuals with sensitive scalp conditions, such as eczema or psoriasis, should use a gentle onion juice formulation to avoid irritation.
