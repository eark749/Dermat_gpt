 Preparing Yerba Mate Hair Rinses at Home

Making yerba mate hair rinses at home is a simple and effective way to incorporate this beneficial herb into your daily hair care regimen. Here's how to make yerba mate hair rinses at home: 

  • Ingredients: Yerba mate tea bags or loose yerba mate leaves, Water. Optional: Essential oils (such as lavender or rosemary) for added fragrance and benefits

  • Brew Yerba Mate Tea: Bring water to a boil in a pot or kettle. Place yerba mate tea bags or loose leaves in a heatproof container. Pour the boiling water over the yerba mate, covering it completely. Let the yerba mate steep for 10-15 minutes to extract its beneficial properties.

  • Cool the Yerba Mate Tea: Allow the yerba mate tea to cool to room temperature or until it is comfortable to touch. You can speed up the cooling process by placing the container in the refrigerator for a few minutes.

  • Strain (If Using Loose Leaves): If you used loose yerba mate leaves, strain the tea to remove the leaves, leaving only the liquid.

  • Optional: Add Essential Oils: If desired, add a few drops of essential oils such as lavender or rosemary to the yerba mate tea for added fragrance and hair benefits. Lavender oil can help soothe the scalp, while rosemary oil may promote hair growth.

  • Rinse Your Hair: After shampooing your hair as usual, pour the cooled yerba mate tea over your hair as a final rinse. Massage the tea into your scalp and hair, ensuring thorough coverage. Leave the yerba mate rinse on for a few minutes to allow the beneficial compounds to penetrate the hair follicles.

  • Final Rinse: Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water to remove any residue. Optionally, you can follow up with a conditioner if desired, although the yerba mate rinse can also act as a conditioning treatment.

  • Style as Usual: Style your hair as usual after rinsing with yerba mate tea. You can let your hair air dry or use a blow dryer if preferred.

  • Repeat Regularly: For best results, incorporate yerba mate hair rinses into your hair care routine regularly. You can use them once or twice a week, depending on your preferences and hair needs.

 Crafting DIY Yerba Mate Hair Masks

Making homemade yerba mate hair masks is an excellent way to reap the benefits of this herb for your hair. Here's an easy recipe to get you started:

  1. Prepare Yerba Mate Tea: Bring a cup of water to a boil, then steep the yerba mate leaves or tea bags for 10-15 minutes. Let the tea cool completely.

  2. Prepare the Avocado: Cut the ripe avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.

  3. Mash Avocado: Using a fork, mash the avocado until it is smooth and creamy. 

  4. Combine ingredients: In a separate bowl, mash the avocado with 2-3 tablespoons of brewed yerba mate tea, honey, and coconut oil. If desired, add a few drops of essential oil for fragrance and extra benefits.

  5. Blend Well: Combine all of the ingredients until you have a smooth, uniform paste. If necessary, add more yerba mate tea to achieve the desired consistency.

  6. Apply to Hair: Divide your hair into sections and apply the yerba mate hair mask evenly from the roots to the ends, focusing on the tips and any areas of dryness or damage.

  7. Massage into Scalp: Using circular motions, gently massage the mask into your scalp to stimulate blood circulation and hair growth.

  8. Leave on and Relax: After applying the mask, cover your hair with a shower cap or towel and let it sit for 30 minutes to an hour to allow the nutrients to penetrate your hair and scalp.

  9. Rinse Thoroughly: After the specified time has elapsed, thoroughly rinse the mask from your hair with lukewarm water. You may need to shampoo your hair to get rid of any residue.

  10. Style as Usual: After removing the mask, return your hair to its normal state. You can air dry your hair or use a blow dryer if you prefer.

  11. Use regularly: For best results, apply this yerba mate hair mask once a week or as needed to nourish and revitalise your hair.

This DIY yerba mate hair mask is packed with nutrients and antioxidants to hydrate, strengthen, and rejuvenate your hair. Customise the mask with your favourite essential oils or other natural ingredients to meet your hair's needs and preferences.
