DIY Mullein Hair Treatments for Home Use

Making DIY mullein hair treatments at home is an excellent way to capitalise on the herb's potential benefits for hair and scalp health. Here are a few simple recipes to try:

  1. Mullein Infused Oil: Combine dried mullein leaves or flowers in a clean glass jar. Cover the mullein with a carrier oil like olive, coconut, or jojoba. Seal the jar tightly and keep it in a cool, dark place for 4-6 weeks, shaking occasionally. After the infusion, strain the mullein through a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth. Transfer the infused oil to a clean storage container. To use, massage the mullein-infused oil into your scalp and hair for at least 30 minutes, preferably overnight, before washing with shampoo.

  2. Mullein Herbal Rinse: Brew a strong mullein tea by steeping dried mullein leaves or flowers in hot water for 30 minutes. Allow the tea to cool before straining out the leaves or flowers. After shampooing and conditioning, apply mullein tea to your scalp and hair as a final rinse.  Leave the herbal rinse in your hair for a few minutes before rinsing it out with cool water.

  3. Mullein and Honey Hair Mask: In a small bowl, combine 2-3 tablespoons of mullein oil and 1 tablespoon of raw honey. Optional: Add a few drops of essential oils such as lavender or rosemary for fragrance and extra benefits. Apply the mixture to damp hair, concentrating on the lengths and ends.  Leave the mask on for 20-30 minutes before rinsing it off with lukewarm water and shampooing normally.

  4. Mullein Scalp Treatment: In a bowl, combine equal parts mullein oil and aloe vera gel. Add a few drops of tea tree oil for its antiseptic properties, if desired. Part your hair and apply the mixture to your scalp, massaging gently.  Leave the treatment on for 15-20 minutes before rinsing it off with cool water and shampooing normally.

  5. Mullein and Apple Cider Vinegar Hair Rinse: Mix equal parts mullein tea (brewed as described above) and apple cider vinegar in a bowl.  After shampooing your hair, apply the mixture to your scalp and hair as a final rinse. Allow it to sit for a few minutes before rinsing it under cool water.
