What is Acacia Collagen?

Acacia Collagen, or Acacia Seyal Gum Extract, is a plant-based substance obtained from the bark of the African Acacia tree. It was traditionally used in African culture for its therapeutic benefits and has now been repurposed for modern cosmetics due to its unique and beneficial attributes.

Acacia Collagen, unlike animal-derived collagen, is plant-based and vegan-friendly, making it a fantastic option for people seeking cruelty-free skincare. It is a powerful, moisturising substance that improves skin texture and appearance.
