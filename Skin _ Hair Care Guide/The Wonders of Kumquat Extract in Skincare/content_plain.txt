What is Kumquat Extract?
Kumquat extract is made from the fruit of the kumquat plant, either the skin or the complete fruit. Kumquats (Fortunella spp.) are small citrus fruits that resemble miniature oranges. They are native to Southeast Asia but are now grown throughout the world. The extract is prepared by crushing or pressing the fruit to liberate its beneficial components.

Sunscreen
: Use a broad-spectrum sunscreen every day to protect your skin from UV rays, which can dry it. To provide extra moisture, use a moisturizing sunscreen product.
