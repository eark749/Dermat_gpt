What are Tocotrienols?

Tocotrienols are a unique subgroup of vitamin E compounds that belong to the family of tocopherols. While tocopherols have long been recognized for their antioxidant capabilities, tocotrienols offer a distinct set of benefits that sets them apart. Tocotrienols possess a more potent antioxidant activity, making them highly effective in combating free radicals and oxidative stress, the major culprits behind premature aging and skin damage.

Sources of Tocotrienols

Tocotrienols can be found in various natural sources, with palm oil being the most abundant. Derived from the fruits of the palm tree, palm oil contains a rich concentration of tocotrienols, making it a valuable ingredient in skincare formulations. Other sources of tocotrienols include rice bran, barley, and annatto seeds. By harnessing these natural sources, skincare products can provide a more holistic and sustainable approach to skin health.
