Tangelo Extract Hair Treatment: Nourish and Revitalize
Tangelo extract can be used to create a nourishing and revitalizing hair treatment that will support the growth of healthier, more vibrant hair. Here's a quick homemade recipe to make use of tangelo extract's hair-benefiting properties:
Tangelo Extract Hair Treatment
: Ingredients are 1 ripe tangelo, 2 tablespoons coconut oil, 1 tablespoon honey, and 5-10 drops of lavender essential oil (optional, for fragrance). Wash the tangelo thoroughly to remove any dirt or residue from the peel. Cut it in half and extract the juice using a citrus juicer or by hand squeezing. Strain the juice to remove any pulp or seeds, leaving behind pure tangelo juice. In a bowl, mix the freshly squeezed tangelo juice, coconut oil, and honey. Stir well until the ingredients are thoroughly combined. If desired, add a few drops of lavender essential oil for a relaxing fragrance. Transfer the mixture to a small saucepan and heat gently over low heat until the coconut oil is melted and the ingredients are well-blended. Be careful not to overheat the mixture, as excessive heat can degrade the beneficial properties of the ingredients. Allow the mixture to cool slightly before applying it to your hair. Apply the treatment to damp hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the ends. Massage the mixture into your scalp and hair, ensuring even distribution. Once applied, cover your hair with a shower cap or towel to trap heat and enhance absorption. Leave the treatment on for 30 minutes to 1 hour to allow the nourishing ingredients to penetrate the hair shaft and scalp. After the treatment period, rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water to remove the treatment. Follow up with your regular shampoo and conditioner if desired. Style your hair as usual, noting the added shine and softness from the tangelo extract treatment. For best results, incorporate this tangelo extract hair treatment into your routine once a week or as needed to nourish and revitalize your hair.

Tangelo Extract Hair Conditioning: Boosting Shine and Strength
Tangelo extract for hair conditioning can help to improve lustre, strength, and overall hair health. Here's a simple DIY tangelo extract hair conditioning remedy for shiny, strong locks:
Tangelo Extract Hair Conditioning Treatment
: Ingredients are 1 ripe tangelo, 2 tablespoons plain yogurt, 1 tablespoon honey, 1 tablespoon olive oil (or coconut oil), and 5-10 drops of rosemary essential oil (optional, for added scalp benefits). Wash the tangelo thoroughly, then cut it in half and extract the juice using a citrus juicer or by hand squeezing. Strain the juice to remove any seeds or pulp, leaving behind pure tangelo juice. In a mixing bowl, combine the freshly squeezed tangelo juice, plain yogurt, honey, and olive oil (or coconut oil). Stir the ingredients together until well blended. If desired, add 5-10 drops of rosemary essential oil to the mixture. Rosemary essential oil is known for its stimulating properties, which can promote scalp health and encourage hair growth. After shampooing, apply the tangelo extract conditioning treatment to damp hair, focusing on the lengths and ends. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to distribute the mixture evenly throughout your hair. Once applied, cover your hair with a shower cap or plastic wrap to trap heat and enhance absorption. Leave the conditioning treatment on for 20-30 minutes to allow the nourishing ingredients to penetrate the hair shaft. After the treatment period, rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water to remove the conditioning mixture. Follow up with a cool water rinse to seal the hair cuticle and enhance shine.
