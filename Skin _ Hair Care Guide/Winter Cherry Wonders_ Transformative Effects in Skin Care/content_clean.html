Ashwangandha is one of the products that boost the production of collagen in the body. Hyperpigmentation and dark spots can occur as a result of increased synthesis of colour producing pigment called Melanin. To combat and minimise hyperpigmentation, melanin production needs to be decreased. Wintercherry aids in controlling the overproduction of melanin in the skin. As a result, the skin appears lighter, brighter and even toned. In addition, Winter Cherry also immensely hydrates the skin, thus keeping it moisturised. As a result, the skin appears soft and smooth.
