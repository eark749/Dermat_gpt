Application Techniques and Precautions

When using hair care products that contain chloroxylenol, it's important to carefully follow the instructions on the label. Following are some general application methods and safety advice:

  1. The majority of hair care products containing chloroxylenol should be applied to damp hair. This can make sure the product is applied evenly and gets to the scalp.
