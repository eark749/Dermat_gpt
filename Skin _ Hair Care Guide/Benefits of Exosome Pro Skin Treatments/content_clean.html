Post-Treatment Care and Results

Exosome skin treatments require post-treatment care to achieve optimal results. Patients are usually advised to avoid direct sunlight and to use a broad-spectrum sunscreen to protect the treated areas from UV damage. Keeping the skin clean and moisturized is also important, but harsh skincare products and exfoliants should be avoided until the skin has completely recovered. Mild swelling, redness, or bruising may occur as a result of the treatment, but these symptoms are usually temporary and can be treated at home with simple procedures such as applying a cold compress to reduce swelling. To reduce the risk of complications, avoid strenuous exercise and prolonged exposure to heat for a few days following treatment. Regular follow-up sessions with the treating specialist can help monitor the skin's progress and address any issues that may arise during the recovery process. These check-ins also allow you to determine whether you require additional treatments or changes to your skincare routine to improve and maintain your results.
