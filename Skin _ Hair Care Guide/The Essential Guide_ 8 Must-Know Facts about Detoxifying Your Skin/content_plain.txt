Impacts on Skin Health

vitamins C

: As the skin adjusts to the detoxification process, it may temporarily produce more oil. This is possible if the skin's natural balance is upset during the detox. Gradual Improvement
: It is important to keep in mind that skin detoxification may not always show its advantages right away.
