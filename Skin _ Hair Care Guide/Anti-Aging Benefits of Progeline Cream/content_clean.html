Using Progeline Cream: Tips for Application and Best Practices

To get the most out of Progeline cream, follow the proper application techniques and best practices. Here are some tips to help you make the most of your Progeline cream:

  1. Cleanse Your Skin: Start by washing your face to remove any dirt, oil, or makeup.  This ensures that the active elements in the Progeline lotion permeate the skin properly.   2. Tone Your Skin (Optional): A toner can help to balance your skin's pH and remove any remaining pollutants.  Apply toner to a cotton pad and gently wipe it across your face, avoiding the eyes.

  3. Apply a Serum (Optional): Serums contain concentrated active compounds that can boost the effectiveness of Progeline cream.  Apply a small amount of serum to your face and neck, gently pushing it into the skin.

  4. Use the Appropriate Amount of Progeline Cream: Too much can be wasteful, while too little may not produce the desired results.  Apply a pea-sized amount of Progeline cream to your forehead, cheeks, nose, and chin.

  5. Gently Massage into the Skin: Gently massage into the skin to enhance circulation and ensure uniform distribution. Use your fingertips to gently massage the cream into your skin in upward and outward circular motions. 

  6. Allow Absorption: Allow the cream to thoroughly sink into your skin before adding any other products. Wait a few minutes until the cream has completely absorbed and your skin is dry to the touch.

  7. Follow with Sunscreen (Morning Routine): Sunscreen protects your skin from UV damage, which can negate the effects of anti-aging products. As the final step of your morning skincare routine, apply a broad-spectrum sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher.
