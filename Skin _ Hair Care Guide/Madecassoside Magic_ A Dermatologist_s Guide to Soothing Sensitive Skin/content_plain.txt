DIY Skincare Tips: Incorporating Madecassoside at Home

:
Ingredients include 1 tablespoon Centella asiatica (Gotu Kola) powder, 1 teaspoon honey, 1 teaspoon aloe vera gel, and 2-3 drops of madecassoside extract or oil. In a small mixing basin, combine all of the ingredients thoroughly. Spread the mixture evenly over your clean face, avoiding the eye area. Leave the mask on for 15 to 20 minutes. : Ingredients are 1-2 drops of madecassoside extract or oil and A cotton swab or clean fingertip. Cleanse your face and pat dry. Madecassoside-infused Moisturizer
: Ingredients are Unscented moisturizer (preferably with a light texture) and 2-3 drops of madecassoside extract or oil. Dispense your desired amount of moisturizer into the palm of your hand. Add 2-3 drops of madecassoside extract or oil. Mix well. Apply the moisturizer to your face and neck, massaging gently until fully absorbed.
Madecassoside-infused Facial Mist
: Ingredients are 2-3 drops of madecassoside extract or oil and 1 tablespoon of distilled water or rose water. Mix the madecassoside extract or oil with the distilled water or rose water in a clean spray bottle. Shake well to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed.
