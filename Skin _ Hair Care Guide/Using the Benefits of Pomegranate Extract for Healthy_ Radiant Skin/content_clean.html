What is Pomegranate Extract?

The fruit of the Punica granatum plant, which is native to the Mediterranean region, is used to make pomegranate extract. The extract is made by processing the fruit's seeds, peel, and arils (small, juicy sacs containing the seeds). Pomegranate extract is high in antioxidants, vitamins, and other beneficial compounds that promote skin health and overall well-being. It is commonly used in skincare products due to its numerous skin benefits.
