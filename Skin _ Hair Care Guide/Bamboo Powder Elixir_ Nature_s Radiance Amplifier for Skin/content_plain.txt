: The skin is a highly intricate organ with multiple layers and components, rather than a simple, uniform layer. Complexity
: The skin's complexity extends to its role in maintaining body homeostasis. It controls temperature, fluid balance, and plays an important role in the immune system's defence against infections.

Sensory Landscape

Cosmetic Importance
