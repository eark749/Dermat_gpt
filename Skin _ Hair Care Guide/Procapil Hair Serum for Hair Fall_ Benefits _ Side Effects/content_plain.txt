Understanding the science behind Procapil
Procapil is a trademarked hair care chemical created by the Sederma corporation. It's commonly used in hair care products, particularly those aimed at promoting hair growth, reducing hair loss, and fortifying hair follicles. Here's a quick rundown of the science behind Procapil:
Composition of Procapil
:
Procapil is a combination of three main active ingredients:
Oleanolic Acid
: Oleanolic acid, derived from olive tree leaves, has been studied for its ability to inhibit the enzyme 5-alpha-reductase. This enzyme is involved in the conversion of testosterone into dihydrotestosterone (DHT), which can contribute to hair loss in people who are predisposed to pattern baldness.
Biotinyl-GHK (biotinyl-tripeptide-1)
: This peptide is a modified form of the amino acid (protein building block) glycyl-histidyl-lysine. It is thought to strengthen hair follicles and stimulate hair growth.
Apigenin
: A flavonoid found in many plants, apigenin is thought to improve microcirculation in the scalp, which may benefit hair follicle health and hair growth.
Mechanism of Action
:
These three components work together to address various factors associated with hair loss and promote healthier hair growth:
DHT Inhibition
: Oleanolic acid's ability to inhibit 5-alpha-reductase aids in the reduction of testosterone conversion to DHT, which can contribute to hair miniaturisation and eventual hair loss in people with androgenetic alopecia (
pattern baldness
).
Hair Follicle Stimulation
: Biotinyl-GHK may strengthen and enhance the structure of hair follicles, promoting the growth of stronger, healthier hair.
Better Scalp Microcirculation
: Apigenin is believed to improve blood flow in the scalp, which may improve hair follicle nutrition and foster an environment that is favourable for hair growth.
Utilisation in Hair Care Products
:
Procapil is frequently found in hair care formulations such as shampoos, conditioners, serums, and treatments that are intended to address hair loss, promote hair growth, and improve overall hair health.
Efficacy and Considerations
:
Procapil's effectiveness varies from person to person, and individual outcomes may be affected by factors such as the underlying cause of hair loss, the severity of the condition, and the regular use of medications containing this substance. While some studies suggest that Procapil can stimulate hair growth and prevent hair loss, further thorough clinical trials may be required to fully evaluate its efficacy. As with any hair care product or ingredient, it's critical to consult a dermatologist or healthcare professional, particularly if you're dealing with severe hair loss or scalp conditions, to determine the best treatment or approach based on individual needs and potential underlying causes of hair loss.
