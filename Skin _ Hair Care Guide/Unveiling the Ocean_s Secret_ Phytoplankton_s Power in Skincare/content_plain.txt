DIY Beauty: Creating Phytoplankton Skincare Treatments at Home
Making your phytoplankton skincare treatments at home can be an enjoyable and rewarding way to reap the benefits of these marine superfoods for your skin. Here are some easy but effective recipes to get you started:
Phytoplankton Face Mask
: Ingredients are 1 tablespoon of phytoplankton powder, 1 tablespoon of raw honey and 1 teaspoon of plain yogurt. In a small bowl, combine the phytoplankton powder, raw honey, and yogurt. Mix until you achieve a smooth paste-like consistency. Apply the mask to clean, dry skin, avoiding the eye area. Leave the mask on for 10-15 minutes. Rinse off with lukewarm water and pat your skin dry. Follow up with your favorite moisturizer.
Phytoplankton Body Scrub
: Ingredients are 1/2 cup of sea salt or sugar, 2 tablespoons of phytoplankton oil (you can infuse phytoplankton powder into carrier oil), 1 tablespoon of coconut oil and Optional: a few drops of essential oils for fragrance (e.g., lavender, peppermint). In a mixing bowl, combine the sea salt or sugar with the phytoplankton oil and coconut oil. If desired, add a few drops of your chosen essential oils for fragrance. Mix the ingredients until well combined and the scrub reaches your desired consistency. While in the shower, gently massage the scrub onto damp skin using circular motions. Focus on areas prone to dryness or roughness, such as elbows, knees, and heels. Rinse off thoroughly with warm water and pat your skin dry.
Phytoplankton Bath Soak
: Ingredients are 1 cup of Epsom salt, 1/4 cup of dried seaweed or kelp flakes, 2 tablespoons of phytoplankton extract (you can infuse phytoplankton powder into carrier oil) and Optional: a few drops of essential oils for aromatherapy (e.g., eucalyptus, citrus). In a mixing bowl, combine the Epsom salt, dried seaweed or kelp flakes, and phytoplankton extract. Add a few drops of your chosen essential oils if desired, mixing well to distribute the fragrance. Store the mixture in an airtight container until ready to use. To use, add a generous scoop of the bath soak to warm running water as you fill your bathtub. Stir the water to dissolve the salts and distribute the phytoplankton extract.   Soak in the bath for 15-20 minutes, allowing the beneficial ingredients to nourish and pamper your skin.
