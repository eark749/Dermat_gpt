Brightening Turmeric Face Mask
: Combine 1 tablespoon plain yoghurt, 1 teaspoon turmeric powder, and 1 teaspoon honey. In a mixing bowl, combine all of the ingredients and stir well. Exfoliating Papaya Face Mask
: ¼ cup ripe papaya, mashed, 1 tablespoon honey and 1 tablespoon oatmeal (ground into a fine powder). Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Apply the mixture to your face, gently massaging in circular motions to exfoliate. Leave the mask on for 10-15 minutes. Rinse off with lukewarm water and pat your face dry. Papaya contains enzymes called papain, which exfoliate dead skin cells and promote cell turnover, revealing smoother, brighter skin. Honey provides moisture and oatmeal soothes and calms the skin.
Hydrating Coconut Oil and Aloe Vera Face Mask
: 1 tablespoon coconut oil, 1 tablespoon aloe vera gel. Mix coconut oil and aloe vera gel until well combined. Apply the mixture to your clean face. Leave the mask on for 15-20 minutes. Rinse off with lukewarm water and pat your face dry. Coconut oil is deeply moisturizing and helps to repair the skin's natural barrier, while aloe vera soothes and calms irritated skin, making this mask perfect for dry or sensitive skin types.
