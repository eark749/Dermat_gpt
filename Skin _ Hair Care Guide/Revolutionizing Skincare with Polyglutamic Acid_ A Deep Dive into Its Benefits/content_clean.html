Understanding Polyglutamic Acid

Polyglutamic acid (PGA) is a naturally occurring biopolymer of the polyamino acid family. It is composed of repeated units of the amino acid glutamic acid linked together by peptide bonds. PGA can be found in a variety of natural sources, including microorganisms, soybeans, and other plant components. It has acquired popularity in the skincare business due to its unique features and possible skin health advantages.
