What is Panthenol?
Panthenol, also known as pro-vitamin B5, is a water-soluble vitamin that is commonly used in skincare products. It's a derivative of pantothenic acid, which is essential for human life and is found in a variety of foods. Panthenol is often used in cosmetic formulations due to its ability to improve skin hydration and elasticity. It's available in two forms: D-panthenol and DL-panthenol, with D-panthenol being the more stable and bioactive form. It's sourced from plants, such as rice, and can also be synthesized chemically.
