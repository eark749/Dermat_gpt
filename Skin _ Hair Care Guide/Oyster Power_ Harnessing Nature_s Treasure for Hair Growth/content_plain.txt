Iron
: Iron deficiency anaemia has been linked to hair loss, and iron supplementation has been proven to boost hair growth in those with iron shortage. Oysters, which contain heme iron, may help to maintain adequate iron levels in the body, perhaps promoting hair development.
Omega-3 Fatty Acids
: Omega-3 fatty acids are anti-inflammatory and promote general scalp health. While there is no direct evidence relating omega-3 fatty acids from oysters to hair growth, a healthy scalp environment is critical for maintaining hair follicles and creating ideal growth conditions.
Protein
: Adequate protein intake is crucial for hair growth, as hair is primarily composed of protein. Oysters provide high-quality protein, which may support the growth and maintenance of healthy hair strands.
