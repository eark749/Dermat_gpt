  • Exfoliation: Regular but gentle exfoliation (1-3 times per week) helps remove dead skin cells, allowing subsequent skincare products to be absorbed better. To promote a smoother complexion, use a mild exfoliant containing ingredients such as glycolic acid or enzymes.

  • Hyaluronic Acid Hydration: Use hyaluronic acid products, such as serums or moisturisers, to intensely hydrate the skin. Hyaluronic acid attracts and holds moisture, plumping the skin and giving it a dewy appearance.

  • Vitamin C Serum for Radiance: Apply a vitamin C serum to the skin to brighten it, even out the tone, and reduce the appearance of dark spots or hyperpigmentation. Vitamin C is also an antioxidant, meaning that it protects the skin from environmental damage.

  • Moisturise Enough: Use a hydrating and nourishing moisturiser that is appropriate for your skin type. Look for ingredients like ceramides, glycerin, or natural oils to help lock in moisture and keep skin supple.

  • Targeted Treatments: Consider incorporating targeted treatments for specific concerns, such as serums containing niacinamide to improve skin texture, reduce redness, and minimise pores.

  • Sun protection: Use sunscreen every day, even on cloudy days. Sunscreen shields the skin from UV rays, which can cause premature ageing, hyperpigmentation, and skin damage. 

  • Hydrating Masks: Use hydrating masks once or twice a week to add moisture and radiance to your skin. Look for masks that contain hyaluronic acid, aloe vera, or ceramides.

  • Patience and consistency: Using a tailored skincare routine on a consistent basis is essential for achieving and maintaining a radiant and hydrated complexion. Results may take some time to appear, so be patient and consistent with your skincare routine.
