It may also help improve circulation to the scalp, ensuring that hair follicles have an adequate supply of nutrients and oxygen.

DIY Hair Treatments with Pea Sprout Extract
Making DIY hair treatments with pea sprout extract can be a fun and natural way to nourish your hair and improve its overall health. Here are a few simple DIY recipes you can try:
Pea Sprout Extract Hair Mask
: Ingredients are 2 tablespoons of pea sprout extract, 1 ripe avocado and 1 tablespoon of coconut oil. In a mixing bowl, mash the avocado until completely smooth. Mix the pea sprout extract and coconut oil into the mashed avocado until you have a creamy paste. Apply the mask to damp hair, focusing on the roots and ends.  Cover your hair with a shower cap or towel, and leave the mask on for 30 minutes.  Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water, then apply shampoo and
conditioner
as usual.
Pea Sprout Extract Scalp Treatment
:
Ingredients are 2 tablespoons of pea sprout extract, 1 tablespoon of jojoba oil and 5 drops of rosemary essential oil.  Mix the pea sprout extract, jojoba oil, and rosemary essential oil in a small bowl. Part your hair into sections, and apply the mixture directly to your scalp using a dropper or your fingertips. Gently massage the scalp for 5-10 minutes to stimulate circulation and promote absorption of the treatment.  Leave the treatment on for at least 30 minutes or overnight for maximum benefits. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, as usual.
Pea Sprout Extract Hair Rinse
:  Ingredients are 1 cup of distilled water, 2 tablespoons of pea sprout extract, and 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar.  Mix the distilled water, pea sprout extract, and apple cider vinegar in a clean spray bottle. After shampooing and conditioning your hair, spray the mixture evenly on your scalp and hair. Gently massage your scalp to distribute the mixture and stimulate circulation.  Leave the rinse on for a few minutes, then rinse thoroughly with cool water.   Style your hair as usual.
These DIY hair treatments containing pea sprout extract can nourish, strengthen, and promote healthier hair growth. Experiment with different recipes and adjust the ingredients to meet your hair's specific needs and preferences.
