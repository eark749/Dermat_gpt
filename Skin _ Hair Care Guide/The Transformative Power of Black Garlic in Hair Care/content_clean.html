Homemade Hair Treatments with Black Garlic Oil

Making homemade hair treatments with black garlic oil is an excellent way to take advantage of its nourishing and strengthening properties for your hair. Here are some DIY recipes to help you get started:

  • Black Garlic Oil Hair Mask: Ingredients are 3-4 cloves of black garlic, 2 tablespoons of coconut oil and 1 tablespoon of honey. Mash the black garlic cloves into a paste using a fork. In a small bowl, mix the mashed black garlic with coconut oil and honey until well combined. Apply the mixture to damp hair, focusing on the lengths and ends. Leave the mask on for 30 minutes to an hour. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water and shampoo as usual.

  • Black Garlic Oil Scalp Treatment: Ingredients are 5-6 cloves of black garlic, 2 tablespoons of olive oil and 5 drops of tea tree oil (optional, for added scalp benefits). Crush the black garlic cloves using a garlic press or mortar and pestle. Combine the crushed black garlic with olive oil and tea tree oil (if using) in a small jar.  Shake the jar well to mix the ingredients thoroughly. Apply the oil mixture to your scalp and massage it gently for a few minutes. Leave the treatment on for at least 30 minutes or overnight for a more intensive treatment. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual to rinse out the oil.

  • Black Garlic Rinse for Shine and Softness: Ingredients are 5-6 cloves of black garlic and 2 cups of water. Crush the black garlic cloves and add them to a pot with 2 cups of water. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes. Allow the mixture to cool completely, then strain out the black garlic solids. After shampooing and conditioning your hair, use the black garlic-infused water as a final rinse. Pour the rinse over your hair, making sure to saturate it thoroughly. Do not rinse out the black garlic rinse with water; leave it in your hair to dry naturally.

These homemade hair treatments with black garlic oil can nourish, strengthen, and revitalise your hair, making it appear and feel healthier and more vibrant. Experiment with these recipes to see which ones work best for your hair type and needs.
