: Exfoliating the skin can help to remove dead skin cells and unclog hair follicles. Use a gentle exfoliating scrub or a chemical exfoliant that contains alpha-hydroxy acids.
Topical treatments

Shaving
: Shaving can cause the hair to become blunt, making it more likely to grow back into the skin.
Dry skin
: When the skin is dry, dead skin cells can accumulate around hair follicles, leading to clogging and ingrown hairs.
Tight clothing
: Tight clothing can cause friction against the skin, leading to irritation and ingrown hairs.
Genetics
: Some people may be more prone to

Hormones
: Hormonal imbalances can lead to an increase in hair growth and a higher risk of ingrown hairs.
