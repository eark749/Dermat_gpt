Alternative Hair Loss Treatments for Women
There are various alternative treatments for female hair loss, each with a unique mechanism of action and varying levels of efficacy. Here's a full description of the many options:

: It is a common over-the-counter topical therapy for hair loss that comes in both 2% and 5% doses. It works by boosting blood flow to the scalp and activating hair follicles. Apply straight on the scalp twice a day.
Spironolactone
: Spironolactone is an oral medication that blocks androgens, which induce hair loss. It is commonly used off-label for female pattern hair loss and hirsutism. It can help ladies with hormonal disorders and high androgen levels.
Oral Contraceptives
: Oral contraceptives (birth control tablets) can help regulate hormones and lower androgens, which can alleviate hair loss caused by disorders such as polycystic ovary syndrome. It is beneficial to ladies who experience hair loss due to hormonal abnormalities.

Hair Transplant Surgery

Low-Level Laser Therapy (LLLT)
