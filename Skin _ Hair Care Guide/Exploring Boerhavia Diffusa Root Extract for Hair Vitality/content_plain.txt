Finasteride (Propecia)

Common side effects of finasteride may include sexual dysfunction, decreased libido, and gynecomastia (enlarged breast tissue) in some individuals.
Hair Transplant Surgery
involves harvesting hair follicles from donor areas of the scalp (usually the back or sides) and transplanting them to balding or thinning areas. Hair transplant surgery provides a long-lasting and often permanent solution to hair loss, restoring natural-looking hair growth. Hair transplant surgery can be costly and may carry risks such as infection,
