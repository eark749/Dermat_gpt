What is Moringa Oil?
Moringa oil is a natural oil derived from the seeds of the Moringa oleifera tree, also known as the drumstick or ben oil tree. The Moringa oleifera tree is native to Africa and Asia, and it has been used in traditional medicine and skincare for centuries. To extract the oil, the Moringa oleifera tree's seeds are harvested and then sun-dried. The seeds are then crushed to extract the oil, which is usually cold-pressed to retain the nutrients. Moringa oil is a clear, pale yellow oil with a mild, nutty scent and a light, easily absorbed texture. Moringa oil is high in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, making it a popular ingredient in skincare products. It works well on all skin types, including dry, oily, and sensitive skin.
