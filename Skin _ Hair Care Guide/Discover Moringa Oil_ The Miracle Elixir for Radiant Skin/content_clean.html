What is Moringa Oil?

Moringa oil is a natural oil derived from the seeds of the Moringa oleifera tree, also known as the drumstick or ben oil tree. The Moringa oleifera tree is native to Africa and Asia, and it has been used in traditional medicine and skincare for centuries. To extract the oil, the Moringa oleifera tree's seeds are harvested and then sun-dried. The seeds are then crushed to extract the oil, which is usually cold-pressed to retain the nutrients. Moringa oil is a clear, pale yellow oil with a mild, nutty scent and a light, easily absorbed texture. Moringa oil is high in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, making it a popular ingredient in skincare products. It works well on all skin types, including dry, oily, and sensitive skin.

Tips for Maximising the Benefits of Moringa Oil

The following advice will help you get the most out of using moringa oil in your skincare regimen:

  1. Use moringa oil in moderation: Moringa oil is a potent ingredient and a little goes a long way. Start by using a small amount and gradually increase the amount if needed.

  2. Apply moringa oil to damp skin: Applying moringa oil to damp skin can help to lock in moisture and increase absorption.

  3. Use moringa oil as a facial oil: Moringa oil can be used as a facial oil in place of moisturiser. Apply a few drops to your face and neck after cleansing and toning.

  4. Mix moringa oil with other skincare products: You can mix moringa oil with other skincare products, such as moisturisers or serums, to enhance their benefits.

  5. Use moringa oil as a hair and scalp treatment: Moringa oil can also be used as a hair and scalp treatment. Apply a small amount of oil to your scalp and massage it in. Leave it on for at least 30 minutes before washing it out with shampoo.

  6. Store moringa oil properly: Moringa oil should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. This will help to prevent it from going rancid and prolong its shelf life.

  7. Check the quality of the oil: When purchasing moringa oil, look for a high-quality, cold-pressed oil that is free from additives and chemicals. This will ensure that you are getting the full benefits of the oil.
