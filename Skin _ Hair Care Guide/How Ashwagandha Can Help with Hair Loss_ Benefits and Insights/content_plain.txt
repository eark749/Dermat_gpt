Topical Applications: Ashwagandha Hair Masks and Oils
Ashwagandha topical treatments can help to improve scalp health and hair development. Ashwagandha's anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and adaptogenic characteristics make it an excellent supplement to hair care regimens. How to use ashwagandha in hair masks and oils:
Basic Ashwagandha Hair Mask
: Ingredients are ashwagandha powder (1-2 tablespoons) and yogurt or aloe vera gel (2 tablespoons) Mix ashwagandha powder with yogurt or aloe vera gel to form a smooth paste. Apply the mask to your scalp and hair (Leave for 20-30 minutes) and rinse.
Ashwagandha and Coconut Oil Hair Mask:
Ingredients are ashwagandha powder(1-2 tablespoons) and coconut oil (2 tablespoons). Warm the coconut oil slightly and mix it with ashwagandha powder. Add honey if desired and mix well. Apply the mask to your scalp and hair (Leave for 30-60 minutes) and rinse.
Ashwagandha and Olive Oil Hair Mask
: Ingredients are ashwagandha powder (1-2 tablespoons) and olive oil (2 tablespoons). Mix ashwagandha powder with olive oil. Add essential oil if using and mix well. Apply the mixture to your scalp and hair. Leave for 30 minutes and rinse.
Ashwagandha Infused Oil
: Ingredients are ashwagandha powder (2-3 tablespoons) and 1/2 cup of carrier oil (coconut oil or almond oil). Gently heat the carrier oil in a double boiler or microwave until warm. Add ashwagandha powder to the oil and stir well, The mixture sits for 1-2 hours allowing the ashwagandha to infuse into the oil. Strain the oil through a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove the powder.
