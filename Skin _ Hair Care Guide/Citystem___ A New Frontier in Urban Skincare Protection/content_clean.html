Application Tips for Optimal Skin Protection

Consider the following application tips for enhanced effectiveness when using skincare products with Citystem™ or designed to combat environmental stressors:

  • Thoroughly cleanse: Begin with clean hands and a thoroughly cleansed face. To remove dirt, makeup, and pollutants that have accumulated throughout the day or night, use a gentle cleanser appropriate for your skin type.

  • Pat Dry, Do not Rub: After cleansing, gently pat your skin dry with a clean towel. Avoid rubbing your skin, as this can cause irritation, especially if your skin is sensitive.

  • Apply Products to Damp Skin: Apply skincare products, such as serums and moisturisers, to slightly damp skin for better absorption. This aids in the retention of moisture and the penetration of active ingredients.

  • Use Appropriate Amounts: For each product, follow the recommended amount or instructions. Applying too much or too little of the product may reduce its effectiveness.

  • Layer Products Correctly: Products should be applied in the correct order based on their consistency and purpose. To seal in moisture, start with lightweight solutions (like serums) and work your way up to heavier ones (like creams or oils).

  • Gently Pat or Massage: Apply cosmetics with delicate, upward strokes, avoiding forceful tugging or pulling on the skin. Patting or rubbing the goods into the skin promotes absorption and circulation.

  • Target Specific Areas: Pay special attention to areas that are more prone to damage or sensitivity, such as the eyes, neck, and areas exposed to environmental elements. Apply the products liberally to these areas.

  • Allow Absorption Time: Allow each product to absorb for a few moments before applying the next layer. This allows the active ingredients to effectively penetrate the skin.

  • Sunscreen Application: When applying sunscreen, make sure to cover all exposed areas of skin completely. Reapply sunscreen every two hours, especially if you are out in the sun for an extended period of time.

  • Consistency in Application: Seeing results requires consistency. Establish a routine and use the products consistently to reap the most benefits over time.

  • Adapt to Day and Night Routines: Some products, such as daytime moisturisers or serums, may focus more on environmental stressor protection, whereas nighttime products frequently emphasise repair and rejuvenation. Tailor your routine accordingly.

  • Patch Test New Products: Before incorporating a new product completely into your routine, perform a patch test to rule out any adverse reactions or allergies.

By following these application tips, you can maximise the benefits of skincare products containing Citystem™ or formulated to protect against environmental stressors. Consistency and proper application are critical in ensuring that these products provide the intended protection and benefits to your skin.
