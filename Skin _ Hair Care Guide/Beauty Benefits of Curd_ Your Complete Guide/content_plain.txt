Apply the Curd Mask
Use clean fingertips or a brush to apply the curd mixture evenly to your face and neck, avoiding the delicate eye area. To get the most out of the mask, apply a thick layer to your entire face.

Allow the curd mask to sit on your skin for approximately 10 to 15 minutes after application. Take advantage of this opportunity to relax and unwind while the curd works its magic on your skin.
Rinse Off
After the recommended time has passed, remove the curd mask with lukewarm water. Gently massage your skin while rinsing to exfoliate and completely remove the mask.

After rinsing, dry your skin with a clean towel. Apply your favourite moisturiser to lock in hydration and keep your skin soft and supple.
