DIY Hair Masks with Rose of Jericho

Making DIY hair masks with Rose of Jericho can be an enjoyable and natural way to nourish and hydrate your hair. Here are some basic recipes to get you started:

Apply the mask to clean, damp hair, concentrating on the lengths and tips. Cover your hair with a shower cap or towel and leave the mask on for 30 to 60 minutes.

  • Rose of Jericho Scalp Soothing Mask: Ingredients are 1 tablespoon of Rose of Jericho oil, 2 tablespoons of aloe vera gel and 1 teaspoon of tea tree oil. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl until well combined. Part your hair into sections and apply the mask directly to your scalp, massaging gently to stimulate circulation. Leave the mask on for 15-30 minutes.

  • Rose of Jericho Revitalizing Hair Mask: Ingredients are 1 tablespoon of Rose of Jericho extract or powder, 1 ripe avocado, mashed, 1 tablespoon of olive oil and 1 tablespoon of honey. Combine all the ingredients in a blender or mixing bowl until you achieve a smooth consistency. Apply the mask to clean, damp hair, focusing on the roots and ends. Cover your hair with a shower cap or towel and leave the mask on for 30-60 minutes.

These DIY hair masks will hydrate, nourish, and revitalise your hair, making it softer, smoother, and more manageable. Experiment with various ingredients and ratios to determine the best combination for your hair type and needs.
