  • Capricorn:  Ingredients are 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 tablespoon honey, 1 mashed banana. Nourishes and strengthens hair, promoting resilience and health. Mix ingredients, apply to hair, leave on for 30 minutes, then rinse thoroughly.
