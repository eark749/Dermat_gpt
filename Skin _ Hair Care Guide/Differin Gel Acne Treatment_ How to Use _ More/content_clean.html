Dos and Don'ts for Combining Skincare Products 

When it comes to combining skincare products, there are some rules to follow to ensure that you get the most out of each product while minimising the risk of irritation or other negative side effects. Here are some dos and don'ts when it comes to combining skincare products:

Dos:

  1. Clean your face first: Always begin by cleansing your face before using any skincare products. To remove any dirt, oil, or makeup from your skin, use a gentle, non-comedogenic cleanser.

  2. Layer your products in the right order: The general rule of thumb is to apply products in order of their consistency, starting with the thinnest and working your way up to the thickest. You might, for instance, use a toner, a serum, a moisturiser, and then sunscreen.

  3. Give each product time to absorb: Wait until the previous one has completely penetrated your skin before applying the following.  This makes it possible for each product to carry out its duties without interfering with those of the other products.

  4. Use products that complement each other: Look for skincare items that complement one another, like a cleanser and moisturiser from the same brand. This can make sure the products are made to complement one another and are less likely to irritate users or have other unfavourable side effects.

  5. Be consistent: Consistency is key when it comes to skincare. Use your products consistently and as directed to get the most benefit from them.

Don'ts:

  1. Don't mix active ingredients: Avoid using multiple products that contain active ingredients, such as retinoids or AHAs, as this can increase the risk of irritation or other negative side effects.

  2. Don't use too many products at once: It may be difficult to tell which products are effective for you if you use too many at once and overwhelm your skin.

  3. Don't over-exfoliate: Excessive exfoliation can harm your skin's moisture barrier, making it more prone to irritation and other negative side effects. Exfoliate gently once or twice a week, and avoid using multiple exfoliating products at the same time.

  4. Don't assume that more is always better:  Using more products than is advised won't always result in better results and may even irritate you or cause other unfavourable side effects.

You can develop a skincare routine that benefits your skin and aids in your achievement of skincare objectives by adhering to these dos and don'ts.
